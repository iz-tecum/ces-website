Who We Are

We are the oldest pre-professional society on campus. Our mission is to create opportunities to engage with renowned economists, professors, and industry professionals and give students the opportunity to explore career paths in the process.

Banking

Investment banking, sales & trading, private equity, and capital markets recruiting.

Consulting

Management and industry-specific consulting, focused on strategy, operations, and problem-solving.

Economics

Economic consulting, academia, research, and data-driven policy and market analysis.

Business

Most diverse bucket — any opportunities not covered by the previous three categories.
